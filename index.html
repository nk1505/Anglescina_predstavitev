What is the Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic castle built in 1066 on the banks of the River Thames.

History about the casle

  • Made to protect against the Anglo-Saxons
  • At first it was wooden inside the Roman walls
  • They later built a 27-meter stone building
  • This main building was called the Great Tower or today the White Tower

Tower like a prison

  • The prison received its first prisoner in 1100
  • Quite a few prominent prisoners were also imprisoned here
  • Only 5 people were beheaded inside the prison, the rest were on Tower Hill
  • The bodies were later buried under the chapel, and the head was planted on a stake and displayed at Tower Bridge
  • The last person died in the castle in 1941
